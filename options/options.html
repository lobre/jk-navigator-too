JK-Navigator-Too Options

With JK-Navigator-Too, you can use the j and k keys to select elements on web pages; for example, to select search results on Google, or news stories on the BBC, or Reddit posts, or whatever.

In addition, the Enter key activates the link associated with the selected element. Selecting search results on Google usually becomes something like j-Enter, or just Enter; browsing Reddit: j-Enter, j-Enter, and so on.
